Game Audio Collab

Key Info

  • Made in Unity with FMod
  • Language - C#
  • Team Size - 6 people
  • Year - 2023

This project was a collaboration between the games students and the audio students. The idea of the projhect was for the students to work together to create a game with the main focus on have audio in the game in diffentr ways. Our game revolvs around the player walking around the jazz club and when they pick up an instrument, it gets put on th emain stage and becomes a part of the background track.

In the team I had the responsiblity to program the fuctionality, such as moving the character and picking up the objects. I was also added the footstep sounds effectes into the game and made diffent areas of the map have differing amount od echo and reverb. I was also able to create my own background track, and added it into the world which you can listen to in the video above, my backgropund track was not aprt of the final build,but it was fun expermenting with somthing new.
